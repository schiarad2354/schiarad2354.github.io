Backtesting Results for Each State 2022

We employ the Vector Autoregressive (VAR) model to forecast the significant-cybercrime associated covariates and betas for each state. We assess the accuracy and reliability of our forecasting model. The model was fitted using data from 2013 to 2021, and we compared the forecasted values for 2022 with the observed values for that year. The darker shades indicate larger differences between forecasted and observed values.

The average percent difference is 9.5\%, the median percent difference is 8.43\%, the minimum percent difference is 0.461\%, and the maximum percent difference is 29.82\%.
