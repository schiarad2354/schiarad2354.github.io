Cybercrime Severity for 2024

With our fitted forecasting model, we forecast cybercrime severity for each state for year 2024.

Upon closer examination, it becomes evident that California, Colorado, Washington, Texas, and New York exhibit notably high forecasts of cybercrime severity. This underscores the spatial variability in cybercrime severity levels across the U.S., influenced by the diverse regional characteristics and socioeconomic profiles of each state.
